Privacy Policy

Cubloom respects your privacy. This policy explains how information may be collected, used and shared when you use the app.

Information We Collect

Cubloom does not require users to create an account. The app does not directly request names, email addresses, phone numbers, photos, videos, contacts or precise location data.

Advertising

Cubloom may use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, app activity, diagnostics and usage information for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention and service improvement.

Analytics and Diagnostics

The app may use Google or Firebase services for analytics, crash reporting, diagnostics and app performance monitoring.

In-App Features and Purchases

Some rewards, coins, missions or progression features may exist entirely inside the app. If optional in-app purchases are offered, payment information is processed by Apple or Google and is not stored by Cubloom.

Data Deletion

Cubloom does not provide user accounts. To request deletion of data that may be associated with the app, contact us at uygulamadunyasi26@gmail.com.

Children

Cubloom is intended for a general audience and is not specifically directed to children under 13.

データ削除
